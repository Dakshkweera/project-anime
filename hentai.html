Top 5 Hentai/ecchi anime

5.) Harem in the Labyrinth of Another World


  • Rating : 6.3/10
  • episodes: 12
  • creator : Yoshino Akiyama


I mean, I know this is supposed to be borderline hentai ecchi anime, 
but Harem in the Labyrinth in Another World is essentially hentai where 
they didn’t want to put as much effort into a the sex scenes.

It follows the story of a high school student named Takumi who is 
transported to a parallel world where he gains the ability to summon 
magical creatures known as "Familiars." As he navigates this new world, 
Takumi must balance his relationships with the various female characters 
who become his familiars, including his childhood friend, a mysterious 
girl, and a beautiful but dangerous warrior.

4.) Prison school


  • Rating : 7.5/10
  • episodes: 13
  • creator : Ryoji Ikeda


Prison School is a Japanese manga and anime series that revolves 
around a high school student named Hachiman Hikigaya. The story 
begins when Hachiman is compelled to join the Underachievers Club 
in his prestigious high school after inadvertently scoring poorly 
on tests meant to identify the most promising students. The series 
delves into the evolving dynamics and relationships between the club 
members as they navigate the challenges of discipline, humiliation, 
and personal development within the school's strict environment.

The narrative of "Prison School" is renowned for its heavy use of humor, 
often employing irony and absurdity to drive its comedic elements. This 
unique blend of humor and character-driven storytelling sets the series 
apart, offering a distinctive and entertaining portrayal of high school 
life. Originally published in 2011, "Prison School" has garnered a dedicated 
fan base and has been adapted into multiple anime seasons, each comprising 
12 episodes. With its focus on interpersonal relationships, discipline, and 
personal growth, "Prison School" presents a captivating and humorous 
exploration of the high school experience.
